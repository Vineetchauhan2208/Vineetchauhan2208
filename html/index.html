Play your part in reducing the environmental impact of your production process, and report on your progress.

ProcureCircular: a data-driven B2B SaaS platform connecting buyers with accredited suppliers of sustainable and circular materials for their supply-chains. Material which improve the traceability and ‘sustainable’ / ‘circular’ footprint of the end-consumer products.

The organisation works in finding materials including waste and surplus materials with a low to negative carbon footprint and working with brands and manufacturers to create collections with a significantly improved environmental footprint, often saving customers on cost and achieving an improved ESG scoring as a result.
