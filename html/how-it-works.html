How it works

The data-driven B2B SaaS platform connecting buyers with accredited suppliers of sustainable and circular materials for their supply-chains.

ProcureCircular is the software mode of AMBIO, a user can engage with the platform as a Buyer or Supplier, or as both with a single user view of their purchases and sales and the attributed environmental impact.
