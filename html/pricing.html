Become a supplier

A supplier can be a manufacturer who are suppliers of wholesale fabric to list in PC marketplace,or who would like to list a catalogue of material in the secondary market. Additional services include: data verification on their material catalogue, waste audit and cataloging of secondary material and a closing of loop of a selection of waste material.

ESG Consultancy services

Often clients we engage with want to engage with a sustainability initiative and measure the environmental impact they are generating but do not have a broader ESG agenda within which to place the initiative.
